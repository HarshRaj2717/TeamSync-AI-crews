   - Market size and growth potential: The global digital transformation market size was valued at $336.3 billion in 2020 and is expected to grow at a CAGR of 22.6% from 2021 to 2028. (Source: Grand View Research)

   - Market size and growth potential: The global cybersecurity market size was valued at $161.0 billion in 2020 and is projected to reach $366.1 billion by 2028, growing at a CAGR of 11.0% from 2021 to 2028. (Source: Fortune Business Insights)

5. Sustainability and Social Responsibility:
   - Recent developments: ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) investing, circular economy, and green IT are driving the industry towards a more sustainable future.
   - Market size and growth potential: The global green IT market size was valued at $11.3 billion in 2020 and is expected to grow at a CAGR of 15.1% from 2021 to 2028. (Source: Grand View Research)
